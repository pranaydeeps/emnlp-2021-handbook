Abstract#==#In this paper, we describe our system for SemEval-2015 Task 3: Answer Selection
in Community Question Answering. In this task, the systems are required to
identify the good or potentially good answers from the answer thread in
Community Question Answering collections. Our system combines 16 features
belong to 5 groups to predict answer quality. Our final model achieves the best
result in subtask A for English, both in accuracy and F1-score.
