Abstract#==#This paper presents an overview of the system designed and submitted as part of
our partici- pation to the SemEval-2015 Task 10 that deals with Sentiment
Analysis in Twitter. We built a Support Vector Machine (SVM) based su- pervised
learning model for Subtask A (term level task) and Subtask B (message level
task). We also participated in Subtask E viz., deter- mining degree of polarity
with the system built employing the available lexical resources. Ex- periments
with the official datasets show F1 scores of 81.31% and 58.80% for Task A and
Task B, respectively. For Subtask E, our model achieved a score of 0.413 on
Kendalâ€™s Tau metric.
