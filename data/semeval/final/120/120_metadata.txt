Abstract#==#This paper describes the KLUEless system which participated in the SemEval-2015
task on “Sentiment Analysis in Twitter”. This year the updated system based
on the developments for the same task in 2014 (Evert et al., 2014) and 2013
(Proisl et al., 2013) participated in all five subtasks. This paper gives an
overview of the core features extended by different additional features and
parameters required for individual subtasks. Experiments carried out after the
evaluation period on the test dataset 2015 with the gold standard available are
integrated into each subtask to explain the submitted feature selection.
