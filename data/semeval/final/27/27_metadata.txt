Abstract#==#This paper describes the participation of the SINAI research group in the task
Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis of SemEval Workshop 2015 Edition. We propose a
syntactic approach for identifying the words that modify each aspect, with the
aim of classifying the sentiment expressed towards each attribute of an entity.
