Abstract#==#This paper describes the system submitted by the TALN-UPF team to SEMEVAL Task
17 (Taxonomy Extraction Evaluation). We present a method for automatically
learning a taxonomy from a flat terminology, which benefits from a definition
corpus obtained by querying the BabelNet semantic network. Then, we combine a
machine-learning algorithm for term-hypernym extraction with
linguistically-motivated heuristics for hypernym decomposition. Our approach
performs well in terms of vertex coverage and newly added vertices, while it
shows room for improvement in terms of graph topology, edge coverage and
precision of novel edges
