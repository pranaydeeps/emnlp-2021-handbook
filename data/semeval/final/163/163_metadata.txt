Abstract#==#This paper describes our participation in task 14 of SemEval 2015. This task
focuses on the analysis of clinical texts and includes: (i) the recognition of
the span of a disorder mention and (ii) its normalization to a unique concept
identiﬁer in the UMLS/SNOMED-CT terminology. We propose a two-step approach
which relies ﬁrst on Conditional Random Fields to detect textual mentions of
disorders using different lexical, syntactic, orthographic and semantic
features such as ontologies and, second, on a similarity measure and SNOMED to
determine the relevant CUI. We present and discuss the obtained results on the
development corpus and the ofﬁcial test corpus.
