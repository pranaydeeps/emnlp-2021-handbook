Abstract#==#We present the approach followed by INESC-ID in the SemEval 2015 Twitter
Sentiment Analysis challenge, subtask E. The goal was to determine the strength
of the association of Twitter terms with positive sentiment. Using two labeled
lexicons, we trained a regression model to predict the sentiment polarity and
intensity of words and phrases. Terms were represented as word embeddings
induced in an unsupervised fashion from a corpus of tweets. Our system attained
the top ranking submission, attesting the general adequacy of the proposed
approach.
