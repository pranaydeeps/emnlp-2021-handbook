Abstract#==#This paper describes the system submitted by the University of Wolverhampton
and the University of Malaga for SemEval-2015 Task 2: Semantic Textual
Similarity. The system uses a Supported Vector Machine approach based on a
number of linguistically motivated features. Our system performed
satisfactorily for English and obtained a mean 0.7216 Pearson correlation.
However, it performed less adequately for Spanish, obtaining only a mean
0.5158.
