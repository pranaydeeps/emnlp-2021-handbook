Abstract#==#This paper describes QCRI's participation in SemEval-2015 Task 3 "Answer
Selection in Community Question Answering", which targeted real-life Web
forums, and was offered in both Arabic and English. We apply a supervised
machine learning approach considering a manifold of features including among
others word n-grams, text similarity, sentiment analysis, the presence of
specific words, and the context of a comment. Our approach was the
best performing one in the Arabic task and the third best in the two English
subtasks.
