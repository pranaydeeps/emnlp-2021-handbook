Abstract#==#This document presents a brief description about a proceeding research designed for Classification of Sinhala-English code-mixed and code-switched data. Further this contains detailed description about the dataset and the baseline models. The aim of this research is to find the best performing separate models for each task; language identification, hate speech detection, humor detection and sentiment analysis, and a multi tasking model to perform all these classification types. Through this paper, the preliminary steps followed in finding those models are reviewed.
