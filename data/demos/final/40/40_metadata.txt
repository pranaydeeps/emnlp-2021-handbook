Abstract#==#Entity Linking (EL) systems' performance is uneven across corpora or depending
on entity types. To help overcome this issue, we propose an EL workflow that
combines the outputs of several open source EL systems, and selects annotations
via weighted voting. The results are displayed on a UI that allows the users to
navigate the corpus and to evaluate annotation quality based on several
metrics.
