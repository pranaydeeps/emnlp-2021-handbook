Abstract#==#Major depressive disorder is one of the most burdensome
and debilitating diseases in the United
States. In this pilot study, we present a new annotation
scheme representing depressive symptoms
and psycho-social stressors associated with
major depressive disorder and report annotator
agreement when applying the scheme to Twitter
data.
